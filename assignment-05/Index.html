Paper Towns

John Green

Paper Towns is the story of Quentin, a smart teen who tends to follow the rules with his two friends who are of a similar midnset. This went on throughout high school until one night when his neighbour, Margo, who he's in love with, bring him on the adventure of a life time.

Throughout the night, Quentin and Margo run around their city, pulling pranks and just doing everything he would have never thought about, helping Margo get even with her friends who hurt her. This night changed his life, making him think everything would change for him. That was, however, until he woke up the next day to find that Margo had disappeared.

Join Quentin and his friends as they search for Margo, the girl of mystery to everyone, even those who think they know her best
