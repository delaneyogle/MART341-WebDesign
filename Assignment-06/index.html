Butter Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of noodles
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbs of parmesan cheese
  • apline touch to taste

Directions

  1. Cook noodles according to box
  2. Heat butterUntil soft, but not fully melted
  3. Mix noodles, butter, cheese, and apline touch together in the pot
  4. Serve in a bowl right away
